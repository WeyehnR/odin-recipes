Candied Yams

Candied sweet potatoes are a Southern side dish usually served at Thanksgiving and Easter, but delicious anytime. Learn how to make these delicious sweet potatoes the Southern way with this traditional recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 Large Sweet potatoes: Peel and cut six large sweet potatoes into chunks.
  • 1/2 Butter: Cook the sweet potatoes in a stick of butter.
  • 2 cups of light brown sugar: Two cups of light brown sugar sweeten things up a bit.
  • 1 teaspoon of ground Cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ground cloves
  • 1 tablespoon of Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 milk or heavy cream(optional)
  • 1/2 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 5 cups of water

Sweet Potatoes Prep work

  1. Wash all the 6 large sweet potatoes
  2. put 4 cups of water into a large pot
  3. Boil the sweet potatoes in a watered pot for 10 minutes
  4. Drain the water and let the potatoes cool down for 5 minutes
  5. After 5 minutes cooldown, peel each 6 sweet potatoes
  6. Cut the peeled sweet potatoes into your preference of cutting:
    • Slices
    • Dice
    • Small chop
    • Large chop
  7. After you cut them, put them in a oven safe dish in any of these methods:
    • You can use aluminum foil paper to protect the sweet potatoes from sticking to the pan
    • You can spray the pan with cooking oil so that the potatoes does not stick
  8. Set the sweet potatoes aside for now and move on to make the sauce

Sweet Candied Sauce Prep work

  1. Pour 1 cup of water into a medium sized pot
  2. (Optional) Pour 1/2 of milk or heavy cream into a medium sized pot
  3. Bring it to a boil(medium heat) and add in:
    • 1/2 cup of cornstarch
    • 2 cups of light brown sugar
    • 2 cups of light brown sugar
    • 1/2 Butter
  4. Stir your sweet aroma of concotion them together
  5. When it is nice and thick, turn the stove off
  6. Pour the sauce evenly over the sliced potatoes
  7. Pre-heat the oven to 475 degrees
  8. Bake them for 40 mintues
  9. Take the Candied sweet potatoes out of the oven
  10. Then add the following Ingredients and mix them well:
    • 1 teaspoon of ground Cinnamon
    • 1 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
    • 1 teaspoon of ground ground cloves
    • 1 tablespoon of Vanilla extract
  11. Serve your newly sweet candied Yam to enjoy!
