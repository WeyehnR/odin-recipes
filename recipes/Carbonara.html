Spaghetti Cabonara

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara is a rich and creamy pasta dish made with simple ingredients like eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's quick, easy, and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 150g Pancetta (diced)
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 1 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Cracked black pepper
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup Pasta water (reserved)

Pasta Prep work

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water before draining the spaghetti.

Sauce and Assembly

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan, and cracked black pepper until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from heat.
  4. Immediately add the hot, drained spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Quickly pour the egg mixture over the hot pasta, tossing vigorously to coat the spaghetti and create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water if needed to adjust the consistency.
  6. Season with salt to taste and serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan and black pepper.
